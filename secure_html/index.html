NFT Art Hound

Team Blue Spring 2021

Problem statement

Digital artists lack an automated system that prevents stolen art from being sold or auctioned on major NFT marketplaces.

Characteristics

  • Major NFT marketplaces are selling or auctioning copyrighted art (including photography)
  • Artists unfamiliar with copyright laws may not know how to prevent their artwork from being sold or auctioned
  • Artists have to manually fill out copyright claim(s)
  • Artists are losing potential profits
